Matatu FAQ

Common questions about matatu culture

Why are matatus so colorful? +

Matatus are colorful to attract customers and express the owner's style. The designs often feature celebrities, slogans, or religious themes.

What does "Manamba" mean? +

Manamba is the term for the conductor who collects fares and calls out routes. They're known for their energetic style and catchy phrases.

Are matatus safe? +

Modern matatus are generally safe with regulated speed governors. However, it's always important to be cautious and use reputable companies.

Types of Matatus

Learn about different matatu styles in Kenya

Nissan Matatus

The classic Nissan Urvan (14-seater) is the most common matatu. Known for reliability and spaciousness.

Toyota Matatus

Toyota Hiace and Noah models are popular for longer routes. They're comfortable and fuel-efficient.

Modern Matatus

Newer models like the Isuzu NPR and Mercedes Sprinter offer more capacity and modern amenities.
